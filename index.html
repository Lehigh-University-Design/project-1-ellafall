The History of the Internet: net.art

‘Internet Art’ or ‘Net.art’: a form of art specifically conceived through the internet, on the internet, and for the internet.

1980s: Emergence of Digital Art

Artists begin experimenting with digital technologies, exploring the creative potential of computers and networks.

1990: Birth of Net.art

The term "net.art" is coined by artists Vuk Cosic and Josephine Bosma.

Early net.artists focus on using the internet as a medium for artistic expression, often incorporating interactivity and collaboration.

Mid-1990s: Pioneering Works

Artists such as Jodi (Joan Heemskerk and Dirk Paesmans) and Olia Lialina create pioneering net.art works, challenging traditional notions of art and audience participation.

1996: Documenta X

Net.art gains international recognition at Documenta X, an influential contemporary art exhibition in Kassel, Germany, with the inclusion of net.art projects.

Pictured above is Jodi's "My%Desktop", one of the works displayed at the exhibition

1996: My Boyfriend Came Back From the War

Visit the art piece here!

Olia Lialina creates the netfilm My Boyfriend Came Back From the War which tells a nonlinear story of a couple being reunited after a military conflict.

The piece is recognized as a seminal internet artwork

2000s: Evolution and Diversification

Net.art evolves, incorporating a wide range of media, including sound, video, and interactive elements.

Artists like Heath Bunting, Alexei Shulgin, and Natalie Bookchin contribute to the diversification of net.art practices. Above, listen to one of Alexei Shulgin’s most acclaimed installations, an album of pop songs sung through text-to-speech software

2000s: Social Media and Web 2.0

Net.artists respond to the rise of social media and Web 2.0, exploring the implications of these platforms on art and society.

Pictured above is a screengrab of Jodi's GEO GOO, a piece that explores how with services such as Google Maps, our globe has "become accessible as a commercial multi-user interface, designed and managed by an influential global corporation."

Present and Future
